Under Pressure

 Noon is fast approaching and with it, the deadline to have our first video posted.  All the students are working furiously to get their jobs done.  They are an amazing crew!!  Keep up the great work.

Getting up and running. . .

Guess what, just wrote a super blog entry about our travels, set up and current issues.  However, when trying to insert an image, I lost my content.  And honestly it was good.  Rife with quotes from Hamlet, you really would've enjoyed it.  However, such is a semi-charmed techno life.  DO OVER. . .

 So, after a challenging travel day full of rain, snow, calls to department of highways, we all arrived.  Late, but ambitious, our command centre was eventually set up in the Shakespeare Room.  By midnight, we were wondering if we would were meant to be or not to be. . .

 Mrs. Cey gave us an inspiring speech reminding students that  the clothes make the man and above all as journalists, to thine own self, and to the truth, be true.

 So this morning, we are re-routering, re-networking, painfully plodding through content with underwater speed.  Now that we finally have school email, we feel less alienated.  So,  off we go!!  Stay tuned!!
