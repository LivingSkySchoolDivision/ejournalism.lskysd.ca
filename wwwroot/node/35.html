Jamie Mckenzie laptop session

As a student listening to Jamie Mckenzie, I found that he was pretty interesting.

Jamie talked about how not paying attention interferes with what you're learning, and I agree with him 100%.  He covered a lot of questions for laptops; he talked about what laptops promise and he mentioned what laptops really do.

            Jamie mentioned how they had to work with text books when they were going to school; he says laptops would have been more convenient so they would have really understood what they were doing. I do agree with Jamie because when you work with texts books they have answers in the back of the book and most students would rather look at the answers then use their brain.

            Jamie wants the schools to buy the students laptops, but the school divisions don’t want to lose out on money by buying these laptops for students who only attend school for one or two semesters.
